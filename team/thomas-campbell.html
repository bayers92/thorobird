Thomas Campbell

Managing Member

Thomas Campbell is a founder and principal of Thorobird Companies LLC. Thomas serves as on the Albany Budget and the New York City Budget Committees of the New York State Association for Affordable Housing. He works on the Board of Governors of Triskeles, a youth development nonprofit. A graduate of the Colgate University, he holds both a B.A. in Economics and is an active Member of the Colgate Real Estate Club of New York. He also has a MBA in Real Estate Finance and Entrepreneurship from Columbia Business School.
