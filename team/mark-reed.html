Mark Reed

Director, Chairman

Mark is lead investor and has worked with Thorobird since 2009. Mark is founder of Contact Fund, LLC. He is a former Vice President of Portfolio Management at the Bank of New York where he co-managed a $500 million bond portfolio. Since 1997, Mr. Reed has served on the Board of Directors of Simpson Investment Company, a fourth-generation, family-owned business based in Tacoma, Washington. He also currently serves as Chair of the Board of the Rudolf Steiner School in Manhattan.
